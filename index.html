About

I develop efficient computational methods for modeling and simulation. I have experience with macromolecules (proteins, RNA and DNA), computation of geometric properties both in 2, 3, and higher dimensions, and driving algorithms for self-driving cars.

I completed my Ph.D. studies at Department of Computer Science, University of Copenhagen, in Prof. Pawel Winters lab and at University of California, Santa Cruz, in collaboration with Prof. Kevin Karplus. From 2012-2014 I was a postdoc at INRIA Saclay with Dr. Julie Bernauer and at SLAC Stanford with Dr. Henry van den Bedem. From 2016-2018 I was a postdoc in professors Brian Kobilka and Michael Levitts labs at Stanford University.
